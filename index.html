Bhargava Manja

I'm a recent graduate of UIUC, where I studied mathematics and computer science. My research interests are varied, and my goal is to work on important, hard problems. I am particularly interested in formal methods of software construction, programming language design/implementation, robotic learning, and neuroscience inspired approaches to computer vision. I have conducted research with the FSL and the Bretl Robotics Group. I currently work at Reconstruct.
